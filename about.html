About the Series

The Apothecary Diaries (Kusuriya no Hitorigoto) follows Maomao, a young girl trained in the art of herbal medicine, who is forced to work as a lowly servant in the inner palace.

Though she yearns for an ordinary life, her eccentricity and knowledge of poisons soon draw the attention of Jinshi, a handsome and calculating eunuch.

This fan project is dedicated to providing a clean, accessible way to enjoy both the anime and manga adaptations of this masterpiece.
